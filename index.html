Sample bequest language
Words to guide your advisor conversation

For guidance only — not intended as legal, financial or tax advice. Please consult your attorney.

For US donors

"I give [all of the residue of my estate / an amount equal to X% / USD ___________] to IIT Bombay through IIT Bombay Heritage Foundation, a qualified 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization registered in the State of California, Tax ID 77-0428724, for its general educational and research purposes."

For India donors

"I give [all of the residue of my estate / an amount equal to X% / INR ___________] to IIT Bombay, for its general educational and research purposes."
